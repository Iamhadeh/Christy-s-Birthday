MEET CHRISTIANAH

An ambitious and smart woman, with an abundance of beauty and light, for wherever she sits and stands, she radiates kindness and grace to everyone around. A preacher of patience and hard work, common sense and strength, the woman the bible is talking about when it says in Proverb 31, "she laughs at the day to come, (and) speaks wisdom..."

Christy's job is to feed people and she takes delight in the job so much that she thinks it's her responsibility to give happiness through meals. Her leisure is filled with giggles, lots of giggles, at Instagram memes and TikTok videos. Regularly, she utters a mix-match of Yorubanglish or something like that --- deliberately. She is Christy. This is how she makes everyone laugh.
